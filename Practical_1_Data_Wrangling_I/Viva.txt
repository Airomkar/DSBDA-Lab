
### 🔍 Explanation:

* Convert:


Into:


## 📌 CREATE BINS

```python
bins = np.linspace(min(df["horsepower"]), max(df["horsepower"]), 4)
```

### 🔍 Explanation:

* Divide horsepower into 3 categories:

  * Low
  * Medium
